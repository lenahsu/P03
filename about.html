I'm Elena,

a senior at Simon Fraser University currently majoring in Interactive Arts and Technology and concentrating in interaction design.

I am a visual and experience designer interested in crafting positive experiences and constructing valuable user-centric solutions because I believe in a community that interculturally connects human beings to one another.
