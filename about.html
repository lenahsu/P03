About Me

a visual + ux designer,

a professional pet petter, and a ramen enthusiast in one ☺

Huhlo, there!

I'm Elena,

a senior at Simon Fraser University currently majoring in Interactive Arts and Technology and concentrating in interaction design.

I am an visual and experience designer with an interest in crafting positive experiences and constructing valuable user-centric solutions because I believe in a community in which interculturally connects human beings to one another. Adventure-ready, I like to actively explore new design approaches while striving for engaging and effective design solutions through design, communication, and storytelling.

A few things about me, I am perpetually curious and easily excited, I love to experience new cultures and travel, I am a firm believer of the Oxford comma, and I have a strong dislike for lemon squares. I just came home from a semester abroad in Malmö, Sweden at Malmö University for my studies, and is currently based in Vancouver, Canada for the semester.

Feel free to shoot me a message!
